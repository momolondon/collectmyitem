By booking a service through our website, you agree to these Terms and Conditions.

Customers must provide accurate information when placing a booking, including:

Quotes are calculated based on the information provided. Incorrect information may result in additional charges.

3. Item Descriptions

The price provided is based on the items listed in the booking form.

If additional items are present or items are larger or heavier than described, Collect My Item reserves the right to:

  • Adjust the price
  • Refuse the job
  • Reschedule the service

4. Access and Parking

Customers must ensure safe and reasonable access to the property.

Any additional costs such as:

5. Helpers and Heavy Items

Driver assistance is included for normal loads.

Additional helpers may be required for:

  • Heavy furniture
  • Multiple items

If additional help is required but not booked, the job may be rescheduled or incur extra charges.

Drivers may wait up to 15 minutes at collection and delivery locations. Additional waiting time may result in extra charges.

Maximum liability is limited to £200 per booking unless otherwise agreed.

Fragile items such as mirrors, glass furniture, antiques, and electronics are transported at the customer’s risk unless properly packed.

8. Cancellations

Bookings may be cancelled free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled collection time.

Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a cancellation fee.

If the driver arrives and the job cannot proceed due to incorrect information or lack of access, a call-out fee may apply.

9. Payment

Payment must be made through the website before the service is carried out unless otherwise agreed.

Collect My Item reserves the right to cancel bookings where payment has not been completed.
